British Columbia Grizzly Bear Population Densities

A simple Leaflet web map made as part of a BCIT course. 2018 estimated bear population densities are plotted below in the form of a map, table, and chart. The polygons are composed of Grizzly Bear Population Unit (GBPU) boundaries. Bear density is given in # of bears/1000 km2. Data from BC Data Catalogue
